Our Story

Rooted in
Tradition

In 1935, the Dadwal family of Muradpur village planted their first lychee trees — a quiet beginning that would define the region. Under Raghbir Dadwal, those roots grew into a 20-acre orchard by the 1960s.

Today, the third generation carries that legacy forward: export-grade Shahi and Calcuttia lychees, trusted by wholesalers and exporters across India.
