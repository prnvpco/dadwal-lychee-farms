Our Story

Rooted in
Tradition

In 1935, the Dadwal family of Muradpur village planted their first lychee trees — a quiet beginning that would define the region. Under Raghbir Dadwal, those roots grew into a thriving orchard across decades.

Today, Sidharth Dadwal leads a 40-acre farm — supplying Mumbai, Bengaluru, Ahmedabad and Delhi, with premium Shahi lychees now exported directly to the United Kingdom.
