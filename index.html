Our Story

Rooted in
Tradition

In 1960, the Dadwal family of Muradpur village planted their first lychee trees. Under Rakesh Dadwal, those roots grew into one of Punjab's most reputed lychee orchards.

Today, Sidharth Dadwal leads a 70-acre farm — supplying 10+ cities across India, with premium Shahi lychees exported directly to the United Kingdom and Dubai.

Premium Variety

Shahi
Lychee

The Crown Jewel

Renowned for its exceptionally thick flesh, tiny seed, and intensely floral sweetness. The premier choice for export markets worldwide — prized for shelf life, stunning appearance, and unmatched depth of flavour.

  • Thick Flesh
  • Export Grade
  • Low Seed

The Beloved Classic

Rich heritage and deep sweetness have made Calcuttia the preferred choice for domestic retail and wholesale markets across India. Its vibrant crimson skin and bold flavour are unmistakable.

  • Deep Flavour
  • Vibrant Colour
  • Pan-India
  • High Yield
